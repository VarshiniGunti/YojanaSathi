# Ayushman Arogya Mandir - Health and Wellness Centers

## Category
health, primary healthcare, free medicines, wellness, all categories

## Ministry
Ministry of Health & Family Welfare

## Description
Upgraded health centers providing comprehensive primary healthcare including screening for NCDs, maternal and child health, and free essential medicines.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed, Student

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
1 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Available to all citizens

## Benefits
- Free comprehensive primary healthcare
- Screening for diabetes, hypertension, cancer
- Maternal and child health services
- Free essential medicines
- Teleconsultation facilities
- Yoga and wellness activities
- Health promotion and disease prevention

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card or any identity proof

## Application Process
1. Visit nearest Ayushman Arogya Mandir
2. Register with Aadhaar or identity proof
3. Avail free healthcare services
4. Get free medicines
5. No fees or charges

https://ab-hwc.nhp.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed, Student

## State Applicability
All India
