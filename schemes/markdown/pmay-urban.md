# PM Awas Yojana Urban

## Category
housing, urban, home loan, interest subsidy, EWS

## Ministry
Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs

## Description
Provides financial assistance to urban poor for construction or purchase of house. Offers interest subsidy on home loans and direct grant for house construction.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Street Vendor

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
21 to 70 years

### Income Limit
₹25,000 – ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must live in urban area
- Must not own pucca house in any part of India
- Annual household income below Rs 18 lakh

## Benefits
- Interest subsidy up to Rs 2.67 lakh on home loans
- Direct grant of Rs 1.5 lakh for EWS category
- Loan tenure up to 20 years
- Women ownership or co-ownership mandatory
- Covers new construction and house purchase

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Income Certificate
3. Property documents
4. Bank Account Passbook
5. Caste Certificate if applicable

## Application Process
1. Visit pmaymis.gov.in and register
2. Fill online application with income and property details
3. Upload required documents
4. Submit application and note application number
5. Visit nearest bank for home loan with subsidy

## Official Link
https://pmaymis.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Street Vendor

## State Applicability
All India
