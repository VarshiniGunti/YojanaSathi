# Soil Health Card Scheme

## Category
farmer, agriculture, soil testing, fertilizer, crop productivity

## Ministry
Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

## Description
Provides soil health cards to farmers with information on nutrient status of soil and recommendations on appropriate dosage of nutrients for improving soil health and fertility.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be farmer with cultivable land

## Benefits
- Free soil testing
- Soil health card with nutrient status
- Recommendations for fertilizer application
- Helps reduce fertilizer cost
- Improves crop productivity
- Card issued every 2 years

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Land ownership papers

## Application Process
1. Contact nearest Agriculture Department office
2. Register for soil health card
3. Agriculture officer collects soil sample from your field
4. Sample tested at soil testing laboratory
5. Soil health card issued with recommendations

## Official Link
https://soilhealth.dac.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Farmer

## State Applicability
All India
