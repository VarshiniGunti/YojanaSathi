# PM Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana

## Category
food security, free food grains, ration, COVID relief, BPL

## Ministry
Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution

## Description
Provides additional 5 kg free food grains per person per month over and above the NFSA entitlement. Launched during COVID and continues to support poor families.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
1 to 100 years

### Income Limit
₹10,000 – ₹25,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must have ration card under NFSA
- Automatic eligibility for all NFSA beneficiaries

## Benefits
- Additional 5 kg free food grains per person per month
- Over and above regular NFSA quota
- Completely free — no cost
- Available at Fair Price Shops
- Covers 80 crore beneficiaries

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Ration Card

## Application Process
1. No separate application needed
2. If you have NFSA ration card you are automatically eligible
3. Visit Fair Price Shop with ration card
4. Collect regular quota plus additional 5 kg free
5. Aadhaar authentication at shop

## Official Link
https://dfpd.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed

## State Applicability
All India
