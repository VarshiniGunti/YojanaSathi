# Stand Up India Scheme

## Category
SC, ST, women, entrepreneur, business loan, startup

## Ministry
Ministry of Finance

## Description
Facilitates bank loans between Rs 10 lakh and Rs 1 crore to at least one SC or ST borrower and at least one woman borrower per bank branch for setting up greenfield enterprises.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Self-Employed, Unemployed, Salaried

### Category/Caste
SC, ST

### Age Requirement
18 to 65 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- SC or ST category OR woman entrepreneur
- Must be setting up a new enterprise in manufacturing, services or trading
- Borrower must not be in default to any bank

## Benefits
- Loan from Rs 10 lakh to Rs 1 crore
- Covers 75 percent of project cost
- Repayment period up to 7 years
- Working capital included in loan
- Handholding support and training provided

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. PAN Card
3. Caste Certificate for SC/ST
4. Business Plan
5. Bank Account Passbook
6. Passport size photographs
7. Address Proof

## Application Process
1. Visit standupmitra.in to check eligibility
2. Register on the portal and fill in business details
3. Connect with nearest bank branch through portal
4. Submit business plan and documents to bank
5. Bank processes loan within 45 days

## Official Link
https://www.standupmitra.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Self-Employed, Unemployed, Salaried

## State Applicability
All India
