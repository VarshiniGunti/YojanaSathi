# Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY

## Category
health, insurance, medical, hospital, free treatment

## Ministry
Ministry of Health & Family Welfare

## Description
Worlds largest health insurance scheme providing free health cover of Rs 5 lakh per year to poor and vulnerable families for secondary and tertiary care hospitalisation.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Street Vendor, Unemployed, Homemaker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
1 to 100 years

### Income Limit
₹25,000 – ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be in SECC database or BPL category
- Annual family income below Rs 5 lakh

## Benefits
- Rs 5 lakh health insurance per year — completely free
- Covers 1500+ medical treatments and surgeries
- Valid at government and private empanelled hospitals
- Cashless treatment — no upfront payment needed
- Covers pre and post hospitalisation expenses

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Ration Card
3. Income Certificate
4. Caste Certificate if applicable

## Application Process
1. Visit pmjay.gov.in and check your eligibility by entering Aadhaar
2. If eligible visit nearest Common Service Centre with documents
3. Get your Ayushman Bharat Health Card made for free
4. Show the card at any empanelled hospital for cashless treatment

## Official Link
https://pmjay.gov.in

## State Applicability
All India
