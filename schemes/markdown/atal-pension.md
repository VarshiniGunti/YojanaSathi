# Atal Pension Yojana

## Category
pension, retirement, social security, unorganized sector, old age

## Ministry
Ministry of Finance

## Description
Pension scheme for unorganized sector workers. Guaranteed monthly pension of Rs 1000 to Rs 5000 after age 60 based on contribution amount. Government co-contributes for eligible subscribers.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Street Vendor, Homemaker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 40 years

### Income Limit
₹25,000 – ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must have bank account with Aadhaar linked
- Must not be income tax payer
- Must not be covered under any statutory social security scheme

## Benefits
- Guaranteed monthly pension from Rs 1000 to Rs 5000 after age 60
- Government co-contribution of 50 percent for 5 years for eligible subscribers
- Pension continues to spouse after subscriber death
- Nominee gets corpus amount after both deaths
- Tax benefits under Section 80CCD

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Bank Account Passbook
3. Mobile Number

## Application Process
1. Visit your bank branch with Aadhaar and bank passbook
2. Fill Atal Pension Yojana enrollment form
3. Choose your desired pension amount
4. Monthly contribution auto-debited from your account
5. Can also enroll through net banking or mobile banking
