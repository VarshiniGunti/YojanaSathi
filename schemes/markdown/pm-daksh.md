# PM-DAKSH - Skill Development for Marginalized

## Category
SC, OBC, skill training, marginalized, employment

## Ministry
Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment

## Description
Provides free skill development training to marginalized communities including SC, OBC and minorities. Includes up-skilling, reskilling and entrepreneurship training.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Student, Unemployed, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed

### Category/Caste
OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 45 years

### Income Limit
₹25,000 – ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must belong to SC, OBC or minority community
- Annual family income below Rs 3 lakh for OBC, Rs 2.5 lakh for others

## Benefits
- Free skill training in various trades
- Stipend during training
- Government recognized certification
- Placement assistance
- Entrepreneurship development training
- Digital and financial literacy

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Caste Certificate
3. Income Certificate
4. Educational certificates
5. Bank Account Passbook

## Application Process
1. Visit pmdaksh.dosje.gov.in
2. Register with Aadhaar and caste certificate
3. Select training course
4. Attend training at empanelled institute
5. Get certificate and placement support

## Official Link
https://pmdaksh.dosje.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Student, Unemployed, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed

## State Applicability
All India
