# Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana

## Category
fisheries, aquaculture, subsidy, SC/ST, entrepreneurship

## Ministry
Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying

## Description
Aims to enhance fish production and productivity. Provides financial assistance for fisheries infrastructure, modernization and value chain development.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Self-Employed, Daily Wage Worker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be engaged in fisheries or willing to start
- Can be individual, group or entrepreneur

## Benefits
- 40 to 60 percent subsidy for general category
- 60 percent subsidy for SC/ST
- Support for fish farming, hatcheries, feed mills
- Subsidy for fishing vessels and equipment
- Cold chain and marketing infrastructure
- Skill development and training

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Land or water body documents
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Project report
5. Caste certificate if applicable

## Application Process
1. Visit pmmsy.dof.gov.in
2. Register and submit online application
3. Upload project report and documents
4. Department verifies and approves
5. Implement project and receive subsidy

## Official Link
https://pmmsy.dof.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Farmer, Self-Employed, Daily Wage Worker

## State Applicability
All India
