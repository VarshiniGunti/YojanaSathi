# Lakhpati Didi Scheme

## Category
women, SHG, income generation, skill training, empowerment

## Ministry
Ministry of Rural Development

## Description
Aims to make women SHG members earn Rs 1 lakh or more annually through skill training, credit linkage and market support. Part of women empowerment initiative.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Homemaker, Self-Employed, Daily Wage Worker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
₹10,000 – ₹25,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be member of women Self Help Group
- SHG must be active under NRLM

## Benefits
- Skill training in high-income activities
- Credit linkage up to Rs 20 lakh per SHG
- Market linkages and buyer connections
- Support for value addition and processing
- Handholding and mentoring
- Target to earn Rs 1 lakh or more annually

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. SHG membership proof
3. Bank Account Passbook

## Application Process
1. Join women Self Help Group in your village
2. SHG registers under NRLM
3. Attend skill training programs
4. Access credit for income generation
5. Start enterprise and earn Rs 1 lakh plus annually

## Official Link
https://nrlm.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Farmer, Homemaker, Self-Employed, Daily Wage Worker

## State Applicability
All India
