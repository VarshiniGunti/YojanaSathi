# One Nation One Ration Card

## Category
ration card, portability, migrant workers, food security

## Ministry
Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution

## Description
Enables migrant workers and their families to access subsidized food grains from any Fair Price Shop in India using same ration card.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
1 to 100 years

### Income Limit
₹10,000 – ₹25,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must have NFSA ration card
- Aadhaar must be linked to ration card

## Benefits
- Access food grains from any Fair Price Shop in India
- Useful for migrant workers
- No need to transfer ration card
- Biometric authentication at shop
- Portability across all states

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card linked to ration card
2. Ration Card

## Application Process
1. Ensure Aadhaar is linked to your ration card
2. Visit any Fair Price Shop in India
3. Provide ration card number and Aadhaar
4. Biometric authentication
5. Collect your entitled food grains

## Official Link
https://nfsa.gov.in/portal/onorc

## Target Beneficiaries
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed

## State Applicability
All India
