# PM Awas Yojana Gramin

## Category
housing, home, rural, construction, shelter

## Ministry
Ministry of Rural Development

## Description
Provides financial assistance to rural households to construct a pucca house with basic amenities. A grant of Rs 1.2 to 1.3 lakh is given directly — no repayment needed.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Unemployed, Homemaker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
₹10,000 – ₹25,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must live in rural area
- Must not own a pucca house
- Must be in SECC 2011 list

## Benefits
- Rs 1.2 lakh grant in plain areas — no repayment
- Rs 1.3 lakh grant in hilly and difficult areas
- Additional Rs 12,000 for toilet construction under Swachh Bharat
- 90 days unskilled labour from MGNREGS

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. BPL Ration Card
3. Land ownership document
4. Bank Account Passbook
5. Photograph of existing house

## Application Process
1. Visit your local Gram Panchayat office
2. Ask for PMAY Gramin registration form
3. Panchayat staff will help you fill the form
4. A government officer will visit to verify your housing status
5. After approval money is transferred in instalments as construction progresses

Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Unemployed, Homemaker

## State Applicability
All India
