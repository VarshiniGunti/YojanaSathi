# Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana

## Category
rural youth, skill training, placement, residential training, employment

## Ministry
Ministry of Rural Development

## Description
Skill training and placement program for rural youth. Provides residential training with food and accommodation, certification and guaranteed placement assistance.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Unemployed, Student, Daily Wage Worker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
15 to 35 years

### Income Limit
₹10,000 – ₹25,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be from rural area
- Must be willing to migrate for employment
- Preference to BPL families

## Benefits
- Free residential skill training with food and stay
- Training in high-demand sectors like retail, hospitality, healthcare
- Minimum 70 percent placement guarantee
- Post-placement tracking for one year
- Travel allowance and stipend during training

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. BPL Ration Card or SECC data
3. Educational certificates
4. Bank Account Passbook
5. Passport size photographs

## Application Process
1. Visit ddugky.gov.in to find training partners
2. Contact nearest DDU-GKY training center
3. Register with documents
4. Undergo residential training for 3 to 12 months
5. Get placed in organized sector job

Unemployed, Student, Daily Wage Worker

## State Applicability
All India
