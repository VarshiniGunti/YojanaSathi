# Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana

## Category
insurance, accident, all categories, low cost, bank account

## Ministry
Ministry of Finance

## Description
Accident insurance scheme offering Rs 2 lakh cover for accidental death or permanent disability at just Rs 20 per year premium. Available to all bank account holders aged 18 to 70.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed, Student

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 70 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must have a bank account with Aadhaar linked
- Must be aged 18 to 70 years

## Benefits
- Rs 2 lakh on accidental death or permanent total disability
- Rs 1 lakh on permanent partial disability
- Only Rs 20 per year premium — auto debited from bank
- Renewable every year automatically

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card linked to bank account
2. Bank Account Passbook

## Application Process
1. Visit your bank branch or net banking portal
2. Ask to enroll in Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana
3. Fill the simple consent form
4. Rs 20 will be auto debited from your account every year
5. You are covered immediately after enrollment

## State Applicability
All India
