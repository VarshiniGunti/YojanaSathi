# Digital India Programme

## Category
digital literacy, e-governance, digital services, all categories

## Ministry
Ministry of Electronics and IT

## Description
Umbrella program to transform India into digitally empowered society. Provides digital infrastructure, digital literacy, and digital services to citizens.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed, Student

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
6 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Available to all citizens

## Benefits
- Free digital literacy training through PMGDISHA
- Access to government services online through DigiLocker, UMANG app
- Free WiFi in public places
- Digital payment infrastructure
- E-governance services
- Common Service Centers in villages

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card for digital services
2. Mobile number

## Application Process
1. For digital literacy visit nearest Common Service Centre
2. Register for PMGDISHA training
3. Download DigiLocker and UMANG apps
4. Link Aadhaar for digital services
5. Access government services online

## State Applicability
