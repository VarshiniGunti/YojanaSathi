# PM SVANidhi Street Vendor Loan

## Category
street vendor, loan, working capital, urban, business

## Ministry
Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs

## Description
Provides affordable working capital loans to street vendors to resume their livelihoods that were adversely affected during the Covid pandemic. No collateral required.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Street Vendor, Self-Employed

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 65 years

### Income Limit
₹10,000 – ₹25,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be a street vendor
- Must have vending certificate or letter of recommendation from Town Vending Committee

## Benefits
- Initial loan of Rs 10,000 without collateral
- Enhanced loan of Rs 20,000 on timely repayment
- Further enhanced loan of Rs 50,000 on continued good repayment
- 7 percent interest subsidy per year
- Digital transaction incentive of Rs 1200 per year

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Vending Certificate or Identity Card
3. Letter of Recommendation from Urban Local Body
4. Bank Account Passbook
5. Mobile Number

## Application Process
1. Visit pmsvanidhi.mohua.gov.in or nearest bank
2. Check eligibility and apply online or at bank branch
3. Submit vending certificate and documents
4. Loan processed within 30 days
5. Repay on time to get higher loan next time

## Official Link
https://pmsvanidhi.mohua.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Street Vendor, Self-Employed

## State Applicability
All India
