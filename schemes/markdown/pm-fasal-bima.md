# Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

## Category
farmer, agriculture, insurance, crop, natural calamity

## Ministry
Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

## Description
Crop insurance scheme providing financial support to farmers in case of crop failure due to natural calamities, pests or diseases. Covers all food and oilseed crops.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be a farmer with cultivable land
- Must have taken crop loan from bank
- Must insure crop before sowing season

## Benefits
- Only 2 percent premium for Kharif crops
- Only 1.5 percent premium for Rabi crops
- Full sum insured paid in case of crop loss
- Covers natural calamities, pests and diseases
- Covers post-harvest losses also

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Land ownership papers
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Crop loan documents
5. Sowing certificate from Patwari

## Application Process
1. Visit your bank where you have crop loan
2. Ask for Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana enrollment
3. Fill the form with crop and land details
4. Pay nominal premium amount
5. Insurance active from sowing to harvest
