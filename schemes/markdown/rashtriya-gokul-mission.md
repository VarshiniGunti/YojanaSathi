# Rashtriya Gokul Mission

## Category
farmer, dairy, cattle, animal husbandry, indigenous breeds

## Ministry
Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying

## Description
Aims to conserve and develop indigenous bovine breeds. Provides financial assistance for establishment of integrated cattle development centers and breed multiplication farms.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Self-Employed

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be engaged in dairy farming or animal husbandry
- Must have land for cattle rearing

## Benefits
- Financial assistance for setting up Gokul Grams
- Subsidy for breed improvement programs
- Training on scientific cattle rearing
- Support for fodder development
- Marketing support for milk and milk products
- Veterinary services

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Land ownership papers
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Project report for large units

## Application Process
1. Contact nearest Animal Husbandry Department
2. Submit application with project details
3. Department verifies and approves
4. Implement project as per guidelines
5. Subsidy released in installments

## State Applicability
