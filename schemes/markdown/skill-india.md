# Skill India Mission - PMKVY

## Category
skill training, employment, youth, certification, placement

## Ministry
Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship

## Description
Provides free skill training to youth in various trades with government certification. Includes placement assistance and monetary rewards for successful candidates.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Student, Unemployed, Daily Wage Worker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
15 to 45 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be school or college dropout or unemployed
- Must be willing to undergo skill training

## Benefits
- Free skill training in 40+ sectors
- Government recognized certification
- Average monetary reward of Rs 8000 on successful completion
- Placement assistance after training
- Training duration 150 to 300 hours

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Educational certificates
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Passport size photographs

## Application Process
1. Visit pmkvyofficial.org and find training center
2. Visit nearest training center with documents
3. Enroll in desired trade course
4. Complete training and appear for assessment
5. Get certificate and monetary reward

## Official Link
https://www.pmkvyofficial.org

## Target Beneficiaries
Student, Unemployed, Daily Wage Worker

## State Applicability
All India
