# Jal Jeevan Mission

## Category
water supply, piped water, rural, drinking water, sanitation

## Ministry
Ministry of Jal Shakti

## Description
Aims to provide functional household tap water connection to every rural household. Ensures regular and adequate supply of potable water.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Homemaker, Unemployed

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be from rural area
- Must not have piped water connection

## Benefits
- Free piped water connection to household
- Assured supply of 55 liters per capita per day
- Potable water quality as per BIS standards
- Reduces drudgery of women in fetching water
- Improves health and quality of life

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Address Proof
3. Bank Account Passbook

## Application Process
1. Village level planning done by Gram Panchayat
2. Households identified for connection
3. No individual application needed
4. Connection provided as per village plan
5. Nominal user charges for operation and maintenance
