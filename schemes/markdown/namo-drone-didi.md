# Namo Drone Didi Scheme

## Category
women, SHG, drone, agriculture, entrepreneurship

## Ministry
Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

## Description
Provides drones to women Self Help Groups for agricultural purposes. Enables women to provide drone services to farmers for pesticide spraying and crop monitoring.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Homemaker, Self-Employed

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
₹25,000 – ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be member of women Self Help Group
- SHG must be registered and active

## Benefits
- Drone provided to women SHGs
- Training on drone operation and maintenance
- Opportunity to earn income by providing drone services
- Rs 15,000 grant for pilot training
- Support for business development
- Contributes to women empowerment

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. SHG registration certificate
3. Bank Account Passbook of SHG
4. List of SHG members

## Application Process
1. SHG applies through Agriculture Department
2. Selected SHGs receive drone and training
3. Members get pilot training certification
4. Start providing drone services to farmers
5. Earn income from service charges

## State Applicability
All India
