# National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme

## Category
apprenticeship, skill training, employment, youth, stipend

## Ministry
Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship

## Description
Provides apprenticeship training to youth in industry with monthly stipend. Government shares 25 percent of stipend cost with employers to promote apprenticeship opportunities.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Student, Unemployed

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
14 to 35 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must have passed Class 5 or above
- Must be willing to undergo apprenticeship training

## Benefits
- Monthly stipend from Rs 5000 to Rs 9000 based on qualification
- On-the-job training in real industry environment
- Certificate recognized by Government of India
- Better employment opportunities after completion
- Training period from 6 months to 3 years

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Educational certificates
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Passport size photographs

## Application Process
1. Visit apprenticeshipindia.gov.in and register
2. Create profile with educational qualifications
3. Search for apprenticeship opportunities
4. Apply to suitable positions
5. Attend interview and join training

## Official Link
https://apprenticeshipindia.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Student, Unemployed

## State Applicability
All India
