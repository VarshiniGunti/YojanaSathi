# Nai Roshni - Leadership Development for Minority Women

## Category
women, minority, leadership, empowerment, training

## Ministry
Ministry of Minority Affairs

## Description
Provides leadership development training to women from minority communities. Empowers them with knowledge of government programs, legal rights and confidence building.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Homemaker, Self-Employed, Unemployed

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 65 years

### Income Limit
₹25,000 – ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be woman from minority community
- Preferably from BPL or EWS category

## Benefits
- Free leadership development training
- Information on government schemes and programs
- Legal literacy and rights awareness
- Confidence building and personality development
- Networking opportunities
- Certificate on completion

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Community certificate
3. Income certificate if applicable

## Application Process
1. Contact nearest Minority Welfare Department office
2. Register for Nai Roshni training program
3. Attend training sessions
4. Complete course and get certificate
5. Become community leader and help others

## Official Link
https://minorityaffairs.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Homemaker, Self-Employed, Unemployed

## State Applicability
All India
