# Kisan Credit Card Scheme

## Category
farmer, agriculture, credit, loan, subsidized interest

## Ministry
Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

## Description
Provides short term credit to farmers for cultivation and other needs at subsidized interest rates. Simple and hassle-free credit facility.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 75 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be farmer with cultivable land
- Can be owner cultivator, tenant farmer or sharecropper

## Benefits
- Credit limit based on cropping pattern and scale of finance
- Interest rate only 7 percent per annum
- Additional 3 percent interest subvention on prompt repayment
- Effective interest rate 4 percent
- Accident insurance cover of Rs 50,000
- Life insurance cover up to Rs 50,000

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Land ownership papers
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Passport size photographs

## Application Process
1. Visit nearest bank branch with documents
2. Fill Kisan Credit Card application form
3. Submit land documents
4. Bank verifies and sanctions credit limit
5. KCC issued within 7 to 14 days

## Target Beneficiaries
Farmer

## State Applicability
All India
