# PM Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana

## Category
farmer, pension, old age, social security, small farmer

## Ministry
Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

## Description
Pension scheme for small and marginal farmers. Provides guaranteed monthly pension of Rs 3000 after age 60 with matching contribution from government.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 40 years

### Income Limit
₹25,000 – ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be small or marginal farmer with cultivable land up to 2 hectares
- Must not be income tax payer

## Benefits
- Guaranteed Rs 3000 monthly pension after age 60
- Government contributes equal amount as farmer
- Family pension of Rs 1500 to spouse after farmer death
- Voluntary exit with interest after 5 years
- Nominee gets corpus if both die

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Land ownership papers
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Age proof

## Application Process
1. Visit nearest Common Service Centre with documents
2. CSC operator will check eligibility and enroll you
3. Monthly contribution auto-debited from bank account
4. Contribution ranges from Rs 55 to Rs 200 based on age
5. Pension starts from age 60
