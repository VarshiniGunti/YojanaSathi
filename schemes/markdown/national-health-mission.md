# National Health Mission

## Category
health, free treatment, maternal health, child health, immunization

## Ministry
Ministry of Health & Family Welfare

## Description
Provides free healthcare services including maternal health, child health, immunization, family planning and treatment of communicable diseases at government health facilities.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed, Student

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
1 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Available to all citizens
- Services provided at government health centers

## Benefits
- Free maternal and child health services
- Free immunization for children
- Free family planning services
- Free treatment for TB, malaria, dengue
- Free medicines at government hospitals
- Janani Suraksha Yojana cash assistance for institutional delivery

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Any identity proof

## Application Process
1. Visit nearest Primary Health Centre or Community Health Centre
2. Register with Aadhaar or any identity proof
3. Avail free healthcare services
4. For maternal benefits register pregnancy at health center
5. All services completely free
