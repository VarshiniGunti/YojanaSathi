# Mission Indradhanush - Universal Immunization

## Category
immunization, vaccination, children, health, free vaccines

## Ministry
Ministry of Health & Family Welfare

## Description
Aims to ensure full immunization of all children and pregnant women. Provides free vaccines against 12 vaccine-preventable diseases.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
1 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Children up to 2 years and pregnant women
- Available to all

## Benefits
- Free vaccination against 12 diseases
- Vaccines for diphtheria, pertussis, tetanus, polio, measles, rubella, Japanese encephalitis, hepatitis B, pneumonia, meningitis, rotavirus diarrhea
- Protects children from life-threatening diseases
- Vaccination camps in villages and urban areas
- Tracking through immunization cards

## Required Documents
1. Birth certificate or any age proof
2. Aadhaar Card if available

## Application Process
1. Visit nearest Anganwadi or Primary Health Centre
2. Register child or pregnant woman
3. Get immunization card
4. Attend vaccination sessions as per schedule
5. All vaccines completely free
