# UJALA - LED Bulb Distribution Scheme

## Category
LED bulbs, energy efficiency, electricity saving, all categories

## Ministry
Ministry of Power

## Description
Provides LED bulbs at highly subsidized rates to reduce electricity consumption and bills. Part of energy efficiency mission.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed, Student

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must have electricity connection
- Available to all consumers

## Benefits
- 9 watt LED bulbs at Rs 70 only
- Saves 85 percent electricity compared to incandescent bulbs
- 3 year warranty on bulbs
- Reduces electricity bills significantly
- Environment friendly

## Required Documents
1. Electricity bill
2. Any identity proof

## Application Process
1. Visit nearest UJALA distribution center
2. Show electricity bill and identity proof
3. Exchange old bulbs or buy new LED bulbs
4. Pay Rs 70 per bulb
5. Start saving on electricity bills immediately

Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed, Student

## State Applicability
All India
