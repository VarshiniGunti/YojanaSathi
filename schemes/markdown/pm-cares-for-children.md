# PM CARES for Children Scheme

## Category
children, COVID orphans, education, stipend, health insurance

## Ministry
Ministry of Women and Child Development

## Description
Provides support to children who lost both parents or surviving parent or legal guardian during COVID-19 pandemic. Includes education, health insurance and monthly stipend.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Student

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
1 to 23 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must have lost both parents or surviving parent during COVID-19
- Age below 18 years at time of death of parents

## Benefits
- Rs 4000 monthly stipend from age 18 to 23
- Corpus of Rs 10 lakh at age 23
- Free education from KG to professional courses
- Health insurance of Rs 5 lakh under Ayushman Bharat
- Support for higher education and skill training
- Residential school admission if needed

## Required Documents
1. Death certificate of parents
2. Birth certificate of child
3. Aadhaar Card
4. Bank Account Passbook
5. School enrollment certificate

## Application Process
1. Visit nearest District Child Protection Unit
2. Submit application with death certificates
3. Child Welfare Committee verifies
4. Benefits activated after approval
5. Monthly stipend and education support provided
