# Beti Bachao Beti Padhao

## Category
girl child, education, women, savings, scholarship

## Ministry
Ministry of Women and Child Development

## Description
Scheme to ensure the survival, protection and education of the girl child. Provides financial incentives and educational support for families with girl children.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Unemployed, Homemaker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
1 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must have a girl child
- Girl child must be below 10 years for Sukanya Samriddhi account

## Benefits
- Sukanya Samriddhi Account — 8 percent interest for girl child savings
- Free education for girls up to Class 8
- Scholarship support for higher education
- Conditional cash transfers for girl child education and health

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card of parent
2. Birth certificate of girl child
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Address Proof

## Application Process
1. Visit nearest post office or bank to open Sukanya Samriddhi Account
2. Carry girl child birth certificate and parent Aadhaar
3. Minimum deposit Rs 250 to open account
4. Deposit regularly for maximum benefit
5. For other benefits visit nearest Anganwadi or district office
