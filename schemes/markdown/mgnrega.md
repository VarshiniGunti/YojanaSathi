# Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act

## Category
rural employment, wage employment, 100 days work, livelihood

## Ministry
Ministry of Rural Development

## Description
Guarantees 100 days of wage employment per year to rural households. Provides livelihood security through creation of durable assets in rural areas.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Unemployed, Homemaker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
₹10,000 – ₹25,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be from rural area
- Must be willing to do unskilled manual work

## Benefits
- Guaranteed 100 days employment per household per year
- Wages paid as per state minimum wage rates
- Work provided within 5 km of village
- Unemployment allowance if work not provided within 15 days
- Payment directly to bank account within 15 days

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Bank Account Passbook
3. Passport size photograph
4. Address Proof

## Application Process
1. Visit Gram Panchayat office with documents
2. Apply for MGNREGA job card
3. Job card issued within 15 days
4. Apply for work when needed
5. Work provided within 15 days or unemployment allowance paid

## Official Link
https://nrega.nic.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Unemployed, Homemaker

## State Applicability
All India
