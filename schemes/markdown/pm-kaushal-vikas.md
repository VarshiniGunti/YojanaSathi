# PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana 4.0

## Category
skill training, youth, employment, certification, Industry 4.0

## Ministry
Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship

## Description
Flagship skill development scheme providing industry-relevant skill training to youth. Focus on emerging technologies and Industry 4.0 skills.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Student, Unemployed, Daily Wage Worker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
15 to 45 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be school or college dropout or unemployed
- Must have basic literacy

## Benefits
- Free skill training in 200+ courses
- Training in emerging technologies like AI, IoT, robotics
- Government recognized certification
- Monetary reward up to Rs 8000 on completion
- Placement assistance
- Training duration 150 to 300 hours

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Educational certificates
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Passport size photographs

## Application Process
1. Visit pmkvyofficial.org
2. Find training center near you
3. Enroll in desired course
4. Complete training and assessment
5. Get certificate and monetary reward

## Official Link
https://www.pmkvyofficial.org

## Target Beneficiaries
Student, Unemployed, Daily Wage Worker

## State Applicability
All India
