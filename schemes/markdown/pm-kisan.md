# PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi

## Category
farmer, agriculture, income support, direct benefit

## Ministry
Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

## Description
Direct income support of Rs 6000 per year for small and marginal farmer families with cultivable land. Money is sent directly to bank account in 3 installments of Rs 2000 each.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must own cultivable land
- Must not be an income tax payer
- Must not be institutional landholder

## Benefits
- Rs 6,000 per year directly to bank account
- Paid in 3 installments of Rs 2,000 every 4 months
- No repayment required

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Land Ownership Papers (Khasra/Khatauni)
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Mobile Number linked to Aadhaar

## Application Process
1. Visit pmkisan.gov.in on your phone or computer
2. Click on New Farmer Registration
3. Enter your Aadhaar number and select your state
4. Fill in your land and bank details
5. Submit the form and note your registration number
6. Money arrives in 2 to 4 weeks after verification

Farmer

## State Applicability
