# Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan

## Category
education, student, school, free textbooks, mid-day meal

## Ministry
Ministry of Education

## Description
Integrated scheme for school education from pre-school to class 12. Provides free textbooks, uniforms, mid-day meals and quality education infrastructure.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Student

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
3 to 18 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be enrolled in government or government-aided school

## Benefits
- Free textbooks for all students
- Free uniforms
- Free mid-day meals
- Free bicycles for girls in secondary classes
- Quality education infrastructure
- Teacher training programs
- Special focus on girls education

## Required Documents
1. School enrollment certificate
2. Aadhaar Card
3. Caste certificate if applicable

## Application Process
1. Enroll child in nearest government school
2. Benefits provided automatically by school
3. No separate application needed
4. Mid-day meals provided daily
5. Textbooks and uniforms distributed at start of academic year

## Official Link
https://samagra.education.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Student

## State Applicability
All India
