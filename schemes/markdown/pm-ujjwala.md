# Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana

## Category
women, LPG, gas, BPL, cooking, fuel

## Ministry
Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas

## Description
Provides free LPG gas connections to women from Below Poverty Line households to replace dirty cooking fuels like wood and coal with clean LPG gas.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Homemaker, Unemployed

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
₹10,000 – ₹25,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be a woman
- Must be from BPL household
- Must not already have LPG connection in household

## Benefits
- Free LPG gas connection
- First gas cylinder provided free of cost
- Gas stove provided at subsidised rate
- Subsidy on subsequent cylinder refills

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. BPL Ration Card
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Passport size photograph
5. Address Proof

## Application Process
1. Visit nearest LPG distributor of any oil company
2. Ask for Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana application form
3. Fill form and submit BPL ration card and Aadhaar
4. Connection provided within 15 days of verification
5. Can also apply online at mylpg.in

## Official Link
https://www.pmuy.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Homemaker, Unemployed

## State Applicability
All India
