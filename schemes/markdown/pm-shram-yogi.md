# PM Shram Yogi Maan Dhan Yojana

## Category
pension, unorganized worker, old age, social security

## Ministry
Ministry of Labour and Employment

## Description
Pension scheme for unorganized workers earning up to Rs 15,000 per month. Provides guaranteed monthly pension of Rs 3000 after age 60.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Street Vendor, Homemaker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 40 years

### Income Limit
₹10,000 – ₹25,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Monthly income up to Rs 15,000
- Must not be covered under EPFO, ESIC or NPS
- Must not be income tax payer

## Benefits
- Guaranteed Rs 3000 monthly pension after age 60
- Government contributes equal amount as worker
- Family pension of Rs 1500 to spouse
- Voluntary exit with interest
- Low monthly contribution from Rs 55 to Rs 200

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Bank Account Passbook
3. Self declaration of monthly income

## Application Process
1. Visit nearest Common Service Centre
2. Carry Aadhaar and bank passbook
3. CSC operator will enroll you
4. Monthly contribution auto-debited from account
5. Pension starts from age 60

## State Applicability
All India
