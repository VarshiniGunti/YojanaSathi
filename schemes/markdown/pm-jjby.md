# Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana

## Category
life insurance, death cover, all categories, low cost

## Ministry
Ministry of Finance

## Description
Life insurance scheme offering Rs 2 lakh cover for death due to any reason at just Rs 436 per year premium. Available to all bank account holders aged 18 to 50.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed, Student

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 50 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must have bank account with Aadhaar linked
- Must be aged 18 to 50 years

## Benefits
- Rs 2 lakh life cover for death due to any reason
- Only Rs 436 per year premium
- Auto renewal every year
- Coverage till age 55
- Simple claim process

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card linked to bank account
2. Bank Account Passbook

## Application Process
1. Visit your bank branch or net banking
2. Enroll in Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana
3. Fill consent form
4. Rs 436 auto debited annually from account
5. Coverage active immediately
