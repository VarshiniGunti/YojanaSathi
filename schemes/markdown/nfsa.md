# National Food Security Act

## Category
food security, ration, subsidized food, BPL, Antyodaya

## Ministry
Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution

## Description
Provides highly subsidized food grains to eligible households. Priority households get 5 kg per person per month at Rs 2-3 per kg. Antyodaya households get 35 kg per family.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
1 to 100 years

### Income Limit
₹10,000 – ₹25,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must have ration card
- Must be in priority or Antyodaya category

## Benefits
- Rice at Rs 3 per kg
- Wheat at Rs 2 per kg
- Coarse grains at Rs 1 per kg
- 5 kg per person per month for priority households
- 35 kg per family for Antyodaya households

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Ration Card
3. Income Certificate
4. Address Proof

## Application Process
1. Visit nearest Fair Price Shop or Food and Civil Supplies office
2. Apply for ration card with documents
3. Card issued after verification
4. Collect subsidized food grains monthly from Fair Price Shop
5. Aadhaar authentication required at shop
