# PM POSHAN - Mid Day Meal Scheme

## Category
student, mid-day meal, nutrition, school, children

## Ministry
Ministry of Education

## Description
Provides hot cooked mid-day meals to children in government and government-aided schools. Aims to improve nutritional status and encourage school attendance.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Student

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
3 to 14 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be enrolled in government or government-aided school from pre-primary to class 8

## Benefits
- Free hot cooked meal every school day
- Nutritious meal with prescribed calories and protein
- Improves nutritional status of children
- Encourages school attendance
- Reduces classroom hunger
- Covers 11.8 crore children across India

## Required Documents
1. School enrollment certificate

## Application Process
1. Enroll child in government school
2. Mid-day meal provided automatically
3. No separate application needed
4. Meal served daily during school hours
5. Menu includes rice, dal, vegetables, eggs

## Official Link
https://pmposhan.education.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Student

## State Applicability
All India
