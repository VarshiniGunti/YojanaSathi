# National Scholarship Portal

## Category
student, scholarship, education, SC, ST, OBC, EWS

## Ministry
Ministry of Electronics and IT

## Description
Single portal for students to apply for various central and state government scholarships. Covers pre-matric, post-matric and merit-cum-means scholarships for students from weaker sections.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Student

### Category/Caste
OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
6 to 30 years

### Income Limit
₹25,000 – ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be enrolled in recognized institution
- Family annual income below Rs 2.5 lakh for most schemes
- Must have passed previous examination with minimum marks

## Benefits
- Full tuition fee reimbursement
- Monthly maintenance allowance
- Book and stationery allowance
- Hostel charges for residential students
- Covers studies from Class 1 to PhD level

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Income Certificate from competent authority
3. Caste Certificate
4. Previous year marksheet
5. Bonafide certificate from current institution
6. Bank Account Passbook

## Application Process
1. Visit scholarships.gov.in
2. Register with your Aadhaar number and mobile
3. Select your scholarship scheme from the list
4. Fill application form and upload documents
5. Submit before deadline shown on portal
6. Track status on the same portal

## Official Link
https://scholarships.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Student

## State Applicability
All India
