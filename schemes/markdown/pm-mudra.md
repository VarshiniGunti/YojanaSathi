# Pradhan Mantri Mudra Loan

## Category
business, loan, entrepreneur, self-employed, finance

## Ministry
Ministry of Finance

## Description
Provides loans up to Rs 10 lakh to non-corporate small business owners, shopkeepers, artisans, and entrepreneurs to start or expand their business without collateral.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Self-Employed, Street Vendor, Farmer, Daily Wage Worker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 65 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must have a business plan or existing small business
- No collateral required for loans up to Rs 10 lakh

## Benefits
- Shishu — loans up to Rs 50,000 for new businesses
- Kishore — loans from Rs 50,000 to Rs 5 lakh
- Tarun — loans from Rs 5 lakh to Rs 10 lakh
- No collateral security required
- Low interest rates

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. PAN Card
3. Business Plan or proof of existing business
4. Bank Account Passbook
5. Passport size photographs
6. Caste Certificate if applicable

## Application Process
1. Visit any nationalized bank, regional rural bank, or microfinance institution
2. Ask for Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana loan application
3. Fill the form and attach all documents
4. Bank will verify and process your application
5. Loan amount credited to your account within 7 to 10 days

## Official Link
https://www.mudra.org.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Self-Employed, Street Vendor, Farmer, Daily Wage Worker

## State Applicability
All India
