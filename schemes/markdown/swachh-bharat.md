# Swachh Bharat Mission - Toilet Construction

## Category
sanitation, toilet, rural, urban, health

## Ministry
Ministry of Jal Shakti

## Description
Provides financial assistance of Rs 12,000 for construction of individual household toilets in rural and urban areas to achieve Open Defecation Free India.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Homemaker, Unemployed

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
₹25,000 – ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must not have toilet at home
- Must own house or have permission from landlord

## Benefits
- Rs 12,000 grant for toilet construction
- Technical guidance for construction
- No repayment required
- Improves health and dignity
- Additional benefits under PMAY if eligible

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Bank Account Passbook
3. House ownership proof or landlord permission
4. Photograph of house without toilet

## Application Process
1. Visit Gram Panchayat or Municipal office
2. Apply for Swachh Bharat Mission toilet
3. Submit documents and application
4. Construct toilet as per guidelines
5. Money transferred to account after verification
