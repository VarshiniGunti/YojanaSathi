# National Rural Livelihood Mission

## Category
rural, women, self help group, livelihood, credit

## Ministry
Ministry of Rural Development

## Description
Promotes self-employment and organization of rural poor through Self Help Groups. Provides skill training, credit linkage and market access to rural women.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Homemaker, Unemployed

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
₹10,000 – ₹25,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be from rural area
- Must be willing to join Self Help Group
- Preferably women

## Benefits
- Formation and strengthening of Self Help Groups
- Revolving fund of Rs 15,000 per SHG
- Community Investment Fund of Rs 1.3 lakh per SHG
- Bank linkage for credit up to Rs 10 lakh
- Skill training and market linkages
- Interest subvention on loans

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. BPL Ration Card
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Photograph

## Application Process
1. Contact nearest Block Development Office or Gram Panchayat
2. Join existing Self Help Group or form new group with 10-15 women
3. Register SHG with NRLM
4. Attend training programs
5. Access credit and other benefits through SHG

## Target Beneficiaries
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Homemaker, Unemployed

## State Applicability
All India
