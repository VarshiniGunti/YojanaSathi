# Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana

## Category
bank account, financial inclusion, zero balance, insurance, all categories

## Ministry
Ministry of Finance

## Description
Financial inclusion scheme providing zero balance bank accounts with RuPay debit card, accident insurance cover and overdraft facility to every household.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed, Student

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
10 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must not have any bank account
- Must have Aadhaar or any identity proof

## Benefits
- Zero balance bank account — no minimum balance required
- Free RuPay debit card
- Rs 2 lakh accident insurance cover
- Rs 10,000 overdraft facility after 6 months of satisfactory operation
- Direct benefit transfer of government schemes

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card or any identity proof
2. Address Proof
3. Passport size photograph

## Application Process
1. Visit any bank branch with Aadhaar card
2. Ask for Jan Dhan account opening form
3. Fill simple form and submit documents
4. Account opened immediately
5. RuPay card provided within 7 days

## Official Link
https://pmjdy.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Farmer, Daily Wage Worker, Self-Employed, Salaried, Street Vendor, Homemaker, Unemployed, Student

## State Applicability
All India
