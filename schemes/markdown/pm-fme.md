# PM Formalization of Micro Food Processing Enterprises

## Category
food processing, micro enterprise, subsidy, entrepreneurship

## Ministry
Ministry of Food Processing Industries

## Description
Provides financial, technical and business support to micro food processing enterprises. Aims to upgrade existing units and formalize unorganized sector.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Self-Employed, Farmer

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must have existing food processing unit or willing to start
- Annual turnover below Rs 2 crore

## Benefits
- 35 percent credit-linked subsidy on eligible project cost
- Maximum subsidy Rs 10 lakh per unit
- Training and handholding support
- Marketing and branding assistance
- Access to common infrastructure
- FSSAI license support

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. PAN Card
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Business registration documents
5. Project report

## Application Process
1. Visit pmfme.mofpi.gov.in
2. Register on portal
3. Submit detailed project report
4. Get approval from district level committee
5. Implement project and claim subsidy

## Official Link
https://pmfme.mofpi.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Self-Employed, Farmer

## State Applicability
All India
