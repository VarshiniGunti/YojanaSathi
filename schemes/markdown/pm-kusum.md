# PM-KUSUM Solar Pump Scheme

## Category
farmer, solar pump, renewable energy, subsidy, agriculture

## Ministry
Ministry of New and Renewable Energy

## Description
Provides financial support to farmers for installation of solar pumps and grid-connected solar power plants on barren land. Reduces diesel dependency and electricity cost.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be farmer with cultivable or barren land
- Must have electricity connection for Component C

## Benefits
- 60 percent subsidy on solar pump installation
- 30 percent bank loan at low interest
- Only 10 percent farmer contribution
- Saves diesel and electricity cost
- Additional income from selling surplus power to grid
- 25 year life of solar panels

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Land ownership papers
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Electricity bill if applicable

## Application Process
1. Visit nearest Agriculture or Renewable Energy office
2. Apply for PM-KUSUM scheme
3. Submit land documents and application
4. Empanelled vendor installs solar pump
5. Subsidy credited after installation verification
