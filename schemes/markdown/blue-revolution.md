# Blue Revolution - Fisheries Development

## Category
fisheries, fish farming, aquaculture, subsidy, rural livelihood

## Ministry
Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying

## Description
Integrated scheme for fisheries development. Provides financial assistance for fish farming, hatcheries, cold storage and marketing infrastructure.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Self-Employed, Daily Wage Worker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must have water body or willing to take up fish farming
- Can be individual or group

## Benefits
- 40 to 60 percent subsidy on fish farming infrastructure
- Training on scientific fish farming
- Subsidy for fish seed production
- Support for cold storage and ice plants
- Marketing assistance
- Insurance coverage for fish farmers

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Land or water body ownership proof
3. Bank Account Passbook
4. Project report
5. Caste certificate if applicable

## Application Process
1. Contact nearest Fisheries Department office
2. Submit application with project details
3. Department conducts site inspection
4. Approval and subsidy sanction
5. Implement project and claim subsidy
