# Startup India Initiative

## Category
startup, entrepreneur, business, tax exemption, funding

## Ministry
Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade

## Description
Provides recognition, funding support, tax benefits and easier compliance for startups. Includes access to government tenders and intellectual property support.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Self-Employed, Unemployed, Student, Salaried

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must have innovative business idea
- Company incorporated as private limited or LLP
- Annual turnover below Rs 100 crore

## Benefits
- 3 year income tax exemption
- Self-certification for labor and environment laws
- Access to Rs 10,000 crore fund of funds
- 80 percent rebate on patent filing fees
- Fast track patent examination
- Easier public procurement norms

## Required Documents
1. Certificate of Incorporation
2. PAN Card
3. Business Plan
4. Pitch Deck
5. Recommendation letter if applicable

## Application Process
1. Visit startupindia.gov.in and register
2. Fill startup recognition application
3. Upload incorporation certificate and details
4. Submit for recognition
5. After recognition apply for various benefits

## Official Link
https://www.startupindia.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Self-Employed, Unemployed, Student, Salaried

## State Applicability
All India
