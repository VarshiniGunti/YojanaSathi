# PM Vishwakarma Scheme

## Category
artisan, craftsperson, loan, skill training, traditional occupation

## Ministry
Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises

## Description
Provides financial support and skill training to traditional artisans and craftspeople. Includes collateral-free loans, modern tools and market linkages.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Self-Employed, Daily Wage Worker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
₹25,000 – ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must be traditional artisan or craftsperson
- Working with hands and tools in 18 identified trades

## Benefits
- Collateral-free loan up to Rs 1 lakh at 5 percent interest
- Additional loan up to Rs 2 lakh on timely repayment
- Stipend of Rs 500 per day during training
- Toolkit incentive of Rs 15,000
- Digital transactions incentive
- Marketing support and brand promotion

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Bank Account Passbook
3. Proof of traditional occupation
4. Caste certificate if applicable

## Application Process
1. Visit pmvishwakarma.gov.in
2. Register with Aadhaar
3. Submit application with occupation proof
4. Attend skill training
5. Receive toolkit and apply for loan

## Official Link
https://pmvishwakarma.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Self-Employed, Daily Wage Worker

## State Applicability
All India
