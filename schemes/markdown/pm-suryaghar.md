# PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana

## Category
solar, rooftop, electricity, subsidy, renewable energy

## Ministry
Ministry of New and Renewable Energy

## Description
Provides subsidy for rooftop solar installation in residential houses. Aims to provide free electricity up to 300 units per month through solar power.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Profession
Farmer, Self-Employed, Salaried, Homemaker

### Category/Caste
General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS

### Age Requirement
18 to 100 years

### Income Limit
Above ₹50,000 / month

### Additional Conditions
- Must own residential house
- Must have electricity connection

## Benefits
- 60 percent subsidy for systems up to 2 kW
- 40 percent subsidy for additional capacity up to 3 kW
- Free electricity up to 300 units per month
- Reduces electricity bills to zero
- 25 year life of solar panels
- Environment friendly clean energy

## Required Documents
1. Aadhaar Card
2. Electricity bill
3. House ownership proof
4. Bank Account Passbook

## Application Process
1. Visit pmsuryaghar.gov.in
2. Register with electricity consumer number
3. Apply for rooftop solar with capacity
4. Empanelled vendor installs system
5. Subsidy credited after installation

## Official Link
https://pmsuryaghar.gov.in

## Target Beneficiaries
Farmer, Self-Employed, Salaried, Homemaker

## State Applicability
All India
